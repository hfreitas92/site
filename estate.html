ESTATE

'ESTATE' is the result of cooperation between my Interactive Media program and UM's Theatre department. I helped produce a collaboration that involved turning an interactive play that takes place across a historic estate in Miami into a 360 experience.

The experience involves picking your path around the estate and encountering various characters and their stories (told mainly through monologues) along the way.

How to Download & View Experience



  1. Download the app: First, you’ll need to download the Wonda VR app from the Google Play store or Apple's App Store. Due to the interactivity included in the video, it’s currently only supported by WONDA’s viewer.
    • On iOS: Download the Wonda VR app for Cardboard from the AppStore. Works on iOS 9 and up (from iPhone 5S).
    • On Android: Download the Wonda VR app for Cardboard or GearVR from the Google Play Store. Works on Android 5.0 and up.
  2. Once you download the app, there are two ways to view the experience:
    • QR Code

      or
    • Link

      Navigating to the link opens experience within app. But if you don’t have the app yet when using this link, the link will first lead you to either the Apple App Store or Google Play store (depending on device).

  3. Enjoy the experience :)
